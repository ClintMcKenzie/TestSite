What It Is

The United States Academic Decathlon is a program in which high school students compete in seven areas of academic study and three areas of presentation, along with a team jeopardy event at the end of each competition.  In Kansas, each competing school forms a team and competes in a regional and state competition, and the team that wins the state competition goes to the national competition.

Who We Are

We, as the Academic Decathlon team of Shawnee Mission South, represent our school at a regional competition and a state tournament each year.  Upon winning the state tournament for Kansas (which we have won 22 out of the last 24 years), our team advances to the national tournament, held in one of the competing states around the US.  This year, our team captains were Eleanor Jones and Grace Markum, and our sponsors were Carolyn Cook and Patrick Grant.  We usually meet for about 40 minutes every Tuesday and Thursday to study for the competitions.
